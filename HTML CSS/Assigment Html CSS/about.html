"PESHAWARI SANDALS" - A New Essence Of Elegance & Stylish Footwear

A Peshawari Sandal is known for its softness, unique & eye-catching style. It is a hand-made Sandal, made by the efforts of the talented individuals in Peshawar & Charsadda, KPK, Pakistan. The upper part or front side of the Sandal is made with Pure & Genuine Leather and the bottom part of the Sandal is made with Tyre Sole (thin or thick) depending upon the style of the Sandal. It has many variations in-terms of Colors (i.e. Mustard, Black, Brown etc) and Designs (i.e. Buff, Norozi, Kaptaan Chappal etc).

JM The Sandal House is your one-stop shop for all hand-made trendy, affordable and elegant Peshawari Sandals. We offer great varieties in Peshawari Sandals for your choice & value. You can visit our Store personally or shop online as per your convenience. We are always here at your service. Happy Shopping :)
